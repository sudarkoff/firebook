Starting a fire is easy, right? Why write an entire book about it? Well, if you ever tried lighting a fire on a rainy day, you might know that a simple task of taking a match to a bundle of kindling does not suffice. The task borders on the impossible. And what if you don't have any matches or a lighter with you? Knowing a couple of tricks could turn a cold dark night in the woods into a memorable and fun adventure or even save your ass.

Finally, fire is a fascinating subject in its own right. I had fun learning about it while doing my research for this book and I hope you'll enjoy it too.
